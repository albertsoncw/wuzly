Deciding whether to play as a PMC or a Scav.

When you first enter the world of Tarkov you will be given the option to go in as a PMC (player made character) or a Scav (a player with a random loadout droped in a random location and is friendly with AI). Here we will list below the advantages and disadvantages to each play style below.

PMC

Advantages

  • Gear and loadout customization
  • Keep level xp and stat increases
  • Create a hideout to boost your character's stats

Disadvantages

  • Can lose all loot you enter a raid with
  • AI automatically are agro'd toward you

Scav

Advantages

  • No more gear fear!
  • AI will not attack you unless you attack them first
  • Chance to spawn in with rare and expensive loot

Disadvantages

  • XP is not saved
  • Loadout is usually weak or unbalanced

Conclusion

Whether when you start the game you decide to play as a PMC or a Scav you will have fun either way. Now that you have a list of advantages and disadvantages choose which one you would like to begin with first. As a rule of thumb though, players tend to start as a scav so they can learn the game without fear of losing their precious loot in the process.
